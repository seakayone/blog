EuroSciPy Conference 2016

27 August 2016

:summary:Very short notes on EuroSciPy 2016.

Being a Java and (sometimes) Scala developer on a daily basis I have thought to give it a try and take a look beyond my own nose. Thus, I have been attended the EuroSciPy Conference 2016.

These are some talks I really enjoyed and seem worthy of mention:

  • Gael Varoquaux: Writing Code for Science

  • Thomas Robitaille: Interactive multi-dimensional data exploration and linking with Glue

  • Olivier Grisel: Cluster Computing with Docker

  • Holger Peters: Data Science, Python and the Functional Programming Revolution
